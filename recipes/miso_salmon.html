Miso Salmon

Description

Today I am going to share my tried-and-true easy salmon recipe, Miso Salmon

Miso is made primarily from fermenting soybeans, salt, koji (a fermentation starter), and sometimes rice

Ingredients

  • salmon
  • miso paste
  • mirin
  • sugar
  • salt
    •

    Steps

    1. mix together liquids
    2. marinade salmon with sauce
    3. let sit for 3 hours
    4. preheat over to 350
    5. cook 20-25 mins
    6. remove from oven and enjoy!
