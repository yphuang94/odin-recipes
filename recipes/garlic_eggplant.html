Garlic Eggplant

Description

This is the most garlicy and delicious eggplant you will ever consume!

This is a simple and efficient eastern delicacy beloved by all around the world!

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • salt
    •

    Steps

    1. mince garlic and combine liquids
    2. over high heat, add in garlic, sauce, and eggplant
    3. stir for 10 minutes until sauce thickens
    4. remove from heat and enjoy!
