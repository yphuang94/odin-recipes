Orange Chicken

Description

Today I will be teaching you the culinary favorite of the eastern world, Orange Chicken.

This recipe is made from delicious fried chicken basked in orange cracked sauce.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • sugar
  • soy sauce
  • batter
  • oil
  • seasame seeds

Steps

  1. fry chicken with the batter in hot oil
  2. combine sugar and soy sauce
  3. toss seasame seeds over and remove from heat
  4. Enjoy your delicious culinary favorite!
